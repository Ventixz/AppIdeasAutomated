Frequently asked questions

How often do I refill the water tank?

About once every three weeks for a typical houseplant. The app warns you a few days before it runs low.

Does it work with any plant?

Yes. The plant library covers 200+ common species, and you can create a custom routine for anything else.

Do I need Wi-Fi?

No. Sprout waters and lights on its own. Wi-Fi only adds app notifications and remote stats.
